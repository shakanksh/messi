The Career Timeline

  • 24th June,1987

    Birth Of A Legend

    In The Streets Of Rosario(Argentina), A Boy Named 'Lionel Messi' Was Born To Become A Legend.

  • 29th November,2003

    The First Time For Barcelona

    Lionel Messi Started His FC Barcelona 'C Team'. Not A Long Spell As He Was Soon Promoted To The 'B Team'.

  • 6th March,2004

    The Promotion

    Messi Was Promoted To The Barca 'B Team' And Was On The Eyes Of Selectors And Manager With His Performance.

  • 16th October,2004

    Rise To The First Team

    Messi Got Promoted To The FC Barcelona First Team And Made His La Liga Debut Against Espanyol.

  • 1st May,2005

    The First Of Many

    Messi Scored His First Goal Against Albacete, Assisted By None Another Than The Legend "Ronaldinho". This Was First In The Journey To 700+ Goals.

  • 24th June,2005

    The Historic Signing

    Messi Signed His First Professional Contract For FC Barcelona Continuing His Way To Become The Greatest Player Ever To Play For The Club.

  • 17th August,2005

    Start For The Nation

    Messi Made His Debut For Argentina Against Hungary And Later Becoming The Legend For Argentina Alongside 'Diego Maradona'.

  • 1st March,2006

    Serving The Nation

    Messi Scored His Argentina Goal Against Croatia, Later Becoming The Top Goalscorer For Argentina.

  • 2009

    The Scoring Machine

    Messi Won His First Golden Boot For Scoring 34 Goals In The Season.

  • 1st December,2009

    The Best In The World

    Messi Won His First Ballon D'or For The Giving A World Class Performance.

  • 26th June,2016

    The Goodbye

    Argentina Lost The Copa America Final To Chile At Penalties In A Nail Bitting Match. But The More Shocking News Was That Messi Announced His Retirement From Argentina Football Team.

  • 12th July,2016

    "Don't Go Leo"

    When Messi Announced His Retirement, The Fans All Over The World Specially In Argentina Started A Campaign "Don't Go Leo". Messi Finally Re-Joined The Argentina Squad To Play The 2018 FIFA World Cup.
